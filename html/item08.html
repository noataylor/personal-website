Software Series Emacs

Text editing

As part of my recent dive into the ocean of open source software I have begun using Emacs, 'the fully customizable, extensible, self-documenting text editor'. Whilst I have little expierence writing Lisp code, I have found Emacs' flexibility as a piece of free software highly generative when developing written documents in a variety of languages, and crucial to the performance of complex workflows.

The cover image for this page is a textual rendering of the composed screenshot below which dislays two Emacs clients running in tandem. The displayed buffers show various files and directories relevant to the development of this website. Accordingly, this page illustrates a methodological aspect of the reflexive practices which I am cultivating through the use of free software.
