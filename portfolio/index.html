Hello, I am Noa Taylor

Digital Artist

Contemporary Practices

Coding

I use open soure software throughout my creative practice for the for purpose of furthering my creative and digital freedoms. I have experience with various markup langauges which I utlise to write documents. I am particularly interested in the intersection of coding and performance.

Writing

Writing is an important component of my practice although I would not describe myself as a writer. I am currently exploring the relationship between play and technology and the ways in which writing acts as a critical interface in this context.

Mindfulness

Another crucial element of my creative practice involves mindfulness through meditiation. Accordingly cultural practices of self-care and acceptance are key aspects of my artistic activiies.
